Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

Contact: security@certnode.io. Please encrypt if needed; coordinated disclosure is welcomed.

Scope

  • Primary site: certnode.io, certnode.vercel.app
  • API: api.certnode.io
  • Public JWKS and demo assets under /.well-known and /samples

Guidelines

  • Do not access customer data; limit tests to your own assets.
  • No denial-of-service or stress testing.
  • Respect rate limits; avoid automated scanning that degrades service.

How to report

  1. Send details to security@certnode.io including steps to reproduce and impact.
  2. We'll acknowledge within 3 business days and provide a timeline when possible.
  3. We prefer coordinated disclosure; we'll credit you (optional) once fixed.

Out of scope examples

  • Self-XSS, clickjacking on non-sensitive endpoints, missing SPF/DMARC, rate-limit tuning requests.
