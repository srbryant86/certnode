Security Controls

  • Key Management: ES256 (P-256) via managed KMS/HSM in production; private keys never leave HSM boundary.
  • JWKS: Public keys at /.well-known/jwks.json; KID rotation via alias policy (see log).
  • Transport: HSTS, TLS 1.2+; strict headers; no mixed content.
  • Isolation: Per-tenant scoping; least-privilege IAM; auditable access paths.
  • Retention: We sign your digests; we don't store your documents.
