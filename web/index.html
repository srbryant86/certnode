How It Works

1) Canonicalize & Sign

We apply RFC 8785 JCS and sign with ES256 (P‑256).

2) Receipt

JSON: { protected, signature, payload, kid, payload_jcs_sha256, receipt_id[, tsr] }.

3) Verify Offline

Use our SDK/CLI with published JWKS; no vendor call required.

Benefits

  • Offline verifiability — Verify anywhere with public JWKS.
  • Tamper‑evident receipts — JCS + ES256.
  • Simple to adopt — Zero‑dependency SDKs for Node/Web.
  • Production‑ready — Metrics, rate limiting, provenance releases.
