Technical Specifications

What compliance frameworks do you support?

CertNode supports SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS, GDPR, and custom compliance frameworks. Our cryptographic receipts provide tamper-evident audit trails that satisfy most regulatory requirements.

How quickly can I implement compliance?

Most organizations achieve compliance within 2-4 weeks. Our REST API integrates with existing systems, and our team provides implementation support to accelerate your rollout.

What if I exceed my monthly limit?

We'll alert you at 80% usage and provide options to upgrade. Critical compliance operations continue uninterrupted while we work with you to adjust your plan.

Is there a contract commitment?

No long-term contracts required. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. Enterprise customers can choose annual billing for additional savings and dedicated support.

How secure is my compliance data?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We follow industry security best practices and never store your sensitive business data - only cryptographic signatures and metadata. See our Trust Center for current security status.

Do you provide audit support?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include audit report generation, compliance consulting, and direct auditor support during your compliance reviews.
