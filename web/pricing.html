Frequently Asked Questions

How does usage-based pricing work?
You pay a base monthly fee plus $0.001 per receipt above your plan's included amount. For example, if you're on Professional and generate 600,000 receipts, you pay $200 + (100,000 × $0.001) = $300 total.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What's included in enterprise support?
Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, custom SLAs, priority feature requests, and assistance with compliance requirements.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes, enterprise customers with high volume requirements can receive custom pricing with significant discounts. Contact our sales team for a quote.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
Your service continues uninterrupted. You'll be charged for overage at $0.001 per receipt. We'll notify you when you approach your limits so you can upgrade if needed.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for Developer and Professional plans. Enterprise customers may have implementation fees depending on custom requirements, but these are discussed upfront.
