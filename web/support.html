Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CertNode's platform, implementation, and enterprise features.

How do I get started with CertNode?

Begin with our free trial account to explore the platform. Review the API documentation and use our receipt validator to test functionality. For enterprise deployments, contact our sales team for a personalized demo.

What are the SLA guarantees for enterprise customers?

Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% uptime SLA with 24/7 technical support, dedicated account management, and priority escalation to our engineering team for critical issues.

How does CertNode ensure data security and compliance?

We implement FIPS 140-2 Level 3+ HSM integration, enterprise security frameworks, and comprehensive audit trails. Our platform is designed for SOC 2 readiness, HIPAA compliance, and other regulatory requirements.

Can CertNode integrate with existing enterprise systems?

Yes, CertNode provides RESTful APIs, webhook integrations, and enterprise connectors. Our professional services team can assist with custom integrations and legacy system connections.

What is the typical implementation timeline?

Standard implementations take 2-4 weeks. Complex enterprise deployments with custom integrations may require 6-12 weeks. Our professional services team provides detailed project timelines during planning.

How does pricing work for high-volume usage?

We offer volume discounts for high-volume customers and custom enterprise pricing for specialized requirements. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific usage patterns and pricing needs.
